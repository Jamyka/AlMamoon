Why use Al Mamoon for your insurance needs?

Save on your insurance rates

Not every insurer has the appetite or knowledge to suit you. Our market insight pays dividends. We will assess your needs and make equitable recommendations that serve your interests – not the interests of the insurers.

Get advice from an experienced professional

We provide you with personalized support and can advise you on the coverage options that are best suited to your particular needs. Policies should be worded to fit your needs. Broad, client-specific wordings are our specialty.

Receive faster claims payouts

In the event of a claim or a loss, we will help you through the process. We will be there to answer any questions that come up and, more importantly, to serve as your advocate in ensuring that the payout you receive is fair and delivered to you promptly.

Your risk story, well told

We ask the right questions from first time and effectively sell your risk to insurers. Skillful underwriting presentations are paramount
